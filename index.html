The Question We Ask

"Is it really a 'second chance' if it's the first time you've had real support?"

In the recovery world, "Second Chance" is a buzzword. But labels like that can imply failure.

At HITV, we provide First Chance Support. We don't treat our clients like they are re-starting; we treat them like they are finally starting with the tools—mental health, wellness, and education—that they should have had all along.

Our Mission

HITV cultivates a supportive community for adults and seniors navigating difficult times.

We serve as a central resource hub for those who have been underserved and often overlooked, connecting generations to vital services for education, mental health, recovery, wellness, and stability.

  • Unconditional Dignity
  • Practical Empathy
  • Intergenerational Connection

Our 5 Pillars of Support

We don't just hand out pamphlets. We build a roadmap.

Mental Health

Connecting clients to a vetted network of affordable therapists, psychiatrists, and grief support groups.

Education

GED programs, workforce development for young adults, and digital literacy for seniors.

Recovery

A compassionate "First Chance" pathway with warm handoffs to addiction recovery and re-entry services.

Wellness

Wellness scholarships for gyms, yoga, and nutrition—honoring the mind-body connection.

Stability

Workshops on budgeting, credit repair, and protecting seniors from financial scams.
