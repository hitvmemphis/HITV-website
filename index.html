Our Mission

HITV cultivates a supportive community for adults and seniors navigating difficult times.

We serve as a central resource hub for those who have been underserved and often overlooked, connecting generations to vital services for education, mental health, recovery, wellness, and stability.

  • Unconditional Dignity
  • Practical Empathy
  • Intergenerational Connection
