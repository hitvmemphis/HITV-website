Navigating the Confusion

When you are in a mental health crisis—or when you are grieving the loss of a loved one—the last thing you have energy for is navigating a complicated medical system. Finding a therapist who takes your insurance, understands your story, and has an open slot can feel impossible.

HITV is here to bridge that gap.

We are not a clinic, and we don't just hand you a list of phone numbers. We serve as a concierge for your care. Our Mental Health Committee, led by clinical experts, vets local providers to ensure we are connecting you to safe, effective, and compassionate help.

Clinical Connections

We provide direct referrals to a trusted network of licensed therapists and psychiatrists. We help you understand your options, including sliding-scale and pro-bono services.

Grief & Loss

Losing a loved one changes everything. We connect families and individuals to grief counselors and support groups who understand that grief is a journey, not a problem to be fixed.

Education & Awareness

We believe knowledge is power. We offer resources to help you understand your own mind and body—demystifying diagnosis, medication, and the effects of trauma.
