Rebuilding Your Credit

For many of our clients, a "hard time" ruins their credit score, making it impossible to rent an apartment or get a car. We connect you to:

  • Credit repair workshops.
  • Banking partners for "second chance" accounts.
  • Budgeting mentorship.

Protecting Our Seniors

Financial scams targeting the elderly are an epidemic. We provide education to help seniors protect their fixed incomes.

  • Fraud prevention seminars.
  • Support for navigating Social Security.
  • Safe banking assistance.
