Education & Job Readiness

"It's never too late to learn, and you're never too old to connect."

For Emerging Adults

Without a diploma or job skills, the path to stability is blocked. We connect you to the tools you need to launch a career.

  • GED/HiSET Resources: Connections to adult education centers.
  • Job Readiness: Resume building and interview prep workshops.
  • Trade Referrals: Pathways to local vocational training.

For Seniors

Technology shouldn't leave you behind. We provide patient, respectful support to keep you connected to your family and your world.

  • Digital Literacy: How to use smartphones, Zoom, and email.
  • Safety First: Recognizing online scams and staying safe.
  • Connection: Using tech to fight isolation.
