Health & Wellness

"The body and the mind are connected. You cannot heal one without the other."

Hard times take a physical toll. Stress, trauma, and grief live in the body. At HITV, we believe that physical movement isn't a luxury; it's a vital tool for managing anger, depression, and anxiety.

Wellness Scholarships

We partner with local gyms, boxing clubs, and yoga studios to provide free or subsidized passes for our clients. This provides a safe, disciplined outlet for stress.

Nutrition & Food Security

You can't be mentally stable if you are hungry. We connect families to food pantries and provide practical education on healthy eating on a budget.
