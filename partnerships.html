OMN ENTERTAINMENT

Strategic Advocacy Partner

OldMadeNew Entertainment

Culture meets Care. OMN.ent is a faith-based creative agency and event production company dedicated to bringing people together through the power of music and art.

Through a formal Strategic Alliance (MOU), OMN.ent serves as the primary advocacy platform for HITV. They leverage their concerts, festivals, and artist network to destigmatize mental health and amplify our mission.

"When OMN.ent gathers a crowd, HITV is there to serve them. Together, we ensure that inspiration is always followed by practical support."

URBAN
PROMISE 901

Youth Referral Partner

Urban Promise 901

Because support shouldn't stop when you turn 18. Urban Promise 901 is our trusted partner for youth services in Memphis.

We maintain a dedicated referral pathway: families with children aged 5-22 are connected directly to Urban Promise for mentorship and care. In turn, Urban Promise connects young adults aging out of their programs to the HITV resource hub for continued stability in adulthood.

"A seamless handoff from youth mentorship to adult stability."
