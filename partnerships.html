Community Partners

OMN ENTERTAINMENT

Strategic Advocacy Partner

OldMadeNew Entertainment

OMN.ent is a faith-based entertainment organization dedicated to bringing people together through music and culture. Through our strategic alliance, OMN.ent uses its platform to advocate for HITV's mission, raising awareness and support for mental health in the Memphis community.

They serve as our primary partner for community events, helping us reach those who might not otherwise seek help.

URBAN PROMISE 901

Youth Referral Partner

Urban Promise 901

While HITV focuses on adults and seniors, we know that support must start early. Urban Promise 901 is our trusted partner for youth services.

We maintain a dedicated referral pathway: families with children aged 5-18 are connected directly to Urban Promise for mentorship and care, while young adults aging out of their programs are welcomed into the HITV resource hub for continued stability.
